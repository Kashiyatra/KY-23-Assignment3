Duties of the CA

Campus Ambassador is the face of the Kashiyatra.They act as the link between Kashiyatra and their college and are the first point of contacts for the students.Following are the Responsibulities one is Required to take:

Represent

Represent and Exemplify Kashiyatra in your collrge among your peers

Publicize

Promote Kashiyatra by publicizing its events through social media platforms

Conceptualize

Bring forth ideas and Proposals as valuable input for team Kashiyatra

Organize

Conduct workshops and Promotional Events for Kashiyatra in your College
